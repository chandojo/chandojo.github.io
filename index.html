V+ Beta (formerly Climb Beta)

Finding information and video climbing beta for an area can be time consuming and inconvenient. I created this website to address the needs of climbers who are planning to take a trip outside and want information in one spot by sourcing APIs for weather, maps, and videos across YouTube and Vimeo.
