Events

Climbing Trivia

Monthly trivia night benefitting Seattle's local chapter of the American Alpine Club (AAC)

In conjuction with West Wall Bar and other volunteers, we bring together the local climbing community once-a-month for a fun and thought-provoking trivia. Teams of up to 4 are encouraged to participate. While trivia is primarly climbing-based, we integrate themes each month provided by the previous winner or by current events and celebrations. Winners receive an assortment of prizes.

Visit West Wall Bar's Instagram for future trivia dates: https://www.instagram.com/westwallbar/

Fitness & Sport

Deadlift Fundamental Workshop Series

May 2019 workshop dedicated to teaching the fundamentals of the deadlift

This workshop was dedicated to teaching the fundamentals of the deadlift while offering knowledge and education on how to improve the deadlift and to improve workouts. With a max capacity at 12 participants, each person had the opportunity to learn about this complex movements and its many variations.
